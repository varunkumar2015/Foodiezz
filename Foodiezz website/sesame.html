Uses of Sesame Seeds for Consumers:

Sesame seeds are a versatile and nutritious ingredient that can be enjoyed in various ways by consumers. Some common uses include:

  • Sprinkling on salads and dishes for added flavor and texture
  • Baking into bread, buns, and pastries
  • Ground into tahini paste for dips and sauces

Environmental Benefits of Sesame Plants:

Sesame plants, Sesamum indicum, have sustainable harvesting practices that benefit local communities and the environment.
